OUR CLUB, OUR STORY

Founded in 2025, Kibra Koobs Rugby has grown from a small neighborhood team to a community team. It is more than a sports team; it's a movement of hope in the heart of Kibera, using the rugby pitch to teach respect, discipline, courage, and unity to children aged 5 to 12 years. In a community with limited opportunities, our field becomes a sanctuary where kids do not just learn rugby skills, but grow in confidence, kindness, and resilience. Every scrum builds unity, every tackle builds strength, and every try reinforces that effort leads to reward. Through mentorship and play, we shape future leaders, problem-solvers, and community builders—cultivating a generation that believes in itself and carries the spirit of Koobs forward.

Building Champions On and Off the Field

Kibra Koobs Rugby was established with a vision to provide a safe,inclusive, and empowering environment for Kibera's children to learn rugby, develop life skills, and build character through sports, fostering discipline, teamwork, and self-confidence that extends beyond the playing field.

Our mission is to transform the lives of Kibera's youngest generation through rugby,creating a community where every child—boys and girls alike has the opportunity to discover their potential, develop resilience, and become future leaders both in sport and in life.

We believe in the power of sport to transform lives and communities. Through rugby, we teach respect, teamwork, and perseverance values that help our players overcome challenges both on and off the field.

Volunteer

Donate Equipment

We always need rugby balls, cones, training bibs, first aid kits, and proper rugby boots for our players. Even gently used equipment can make a huge difference.
